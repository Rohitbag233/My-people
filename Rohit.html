Rohit bag (2016)

Hi, I am Rohit from 2016.
Actually, yeh Rohit mein hi hoon the Tiger Rohit.
In 2016, I had a broken hand (Left hand above). It was Wednesday and I was having my train scheduled for Friday,
but the day when I broke my hand, I was in my village and the orthopedic was available only twice a week.
So, I didn't get the treatment that day, but fortunately in my massi's house, there was a neighbor who was an assistant orthopedic doctor
and he did a temporary plaster of my hand. Then after 3 days, a serious doctor did an operation on my hand and after 21 days,
when I recovered, it was found that the operation was not done properly and my hand was a little bit bent. I could fold my hand without any problem,
but keeping my hand free in a normal position made it look bad and ugly. Also, when I walked, it used to look very bad.
So, my parents decided to do a reoperation of my hand and after consulting the doctor, we decided to do the reoperation after 10th grade.
Due to this event, I was mentally very unstable. I was like a tornado of emotions. Due to this, I got bullied many times in my school, tuition,
on the building ground, by people, my friends, and my neighbors.
Due to this, I got disconnected from the outer world. I used to stay at home all the time. I started hallucinating things.
So, I used to talk to myself the whole day.
Then, to overcome my social anxiety, I started going out in front of my building.
Suddenly one day, around 12:45 AM, when I was at the roadside of the garden,
a futuristic supercar came beside me. I had never seen that type of car before.
It was so luxurious; its cost might be 5-6 crore. Then suddenly, a 24-26-year-old man came out and said, "I am Rohit."
Then he told me to sit in his car and have a short drive. Actually, I should not have gone with him, but now I think it was the best decision of my life.
Actually, now I think he came from the future to guide myself.
It was feeling like he was a Rohit, but from the future. Actually, he helped me to find myself and told me the purpose of my life
and also showed me the reality of the world.
Actually, this is so sci-fi, but I don't know what it was. Okay, let's move on to the next.
After that, I started finding a way to achieve my dream goal. I was interested in science. I thought that with expertise in science,
I could achieve those goals. So, I started diving into science for new discoveries.
I started studying articles, documentaries, and following idols in the science field. It was like I was getting out of my depression and
I didn't care about other people's thoughts. Everything was going normal from 7th, 8th grades.
Then, actually, I was working on a traveling technology with my friend named 'SUJAN'.
And he was my first best friend. Actually, he was the first trustworthy person in my life.
